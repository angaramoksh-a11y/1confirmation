The thesis

Decentralizing the web — and society with it.

Since 2017, 1confirmation has backed exceptional founders fueling the decentralization of the web and society. The thesis has not moved: open networks take power that once pooled in institutions and hand it to the masses — in finance, in media, in art.

Culture came first. The firm made early bets on CryptoPunks and backed Beeple, Pak, SuperRare and OpenSea before NFTs had a single headline — because art is how a new technology becomes something people love.

And no maximalism. Bitcoin, Ethereum, Cosmos, Polkadot — chains are instruments, not religions. Conviction over consensus, every time.
